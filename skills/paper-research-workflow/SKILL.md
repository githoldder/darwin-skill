# Paper Research Workflow

## Workflow

1. Define topic, scope, and exclusion criteria.
2. Search across broad and domain-specific channels.
3. Narrow from wide retrieval to core papers.
4. Extract each paper into a structured note: problem, method, data, findings, limitations, and relevance.
5. Compare papers by school, method, application, or timeline.
6. Build a synthesis that explains the field, not just a list of papers.

## Outputs

- search strategy
- candidate list
- core-paper table
- reading notes
- synthesis outline

## Quality Checks

- Sources are relevant and credible.
- Claims in the synthesis map back to extracted notes.
- Research gaps are stated explicitly.

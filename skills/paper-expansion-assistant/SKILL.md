# Paper Expansion Assistant

## Workflow

1. Identify what the paragraph lacks: evidence, comparison, causality, example, or transition.
2. Gather or request the missing source material before expansion.
3. Preserve the original claim while adding new information.
4. Expand through structure, support, and reasoning, not by paraphrasing the same sentence.
5. Check whether every added sentence contributes a new point.

## Expansion Modes

- literature review
- method explanation
- analytical paragraph
- research background

## Quality Checks

- No empty connective prose.
- No unsupported factual inflation.
- The expanded section is denser, not merely longer.

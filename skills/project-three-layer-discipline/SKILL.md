# Project Three-Layer Discipline

## Layers

1. Demand alignment: clarify what is being built, for whom, why it matters, and what remains uncertain.
2. PM 0-to-1 flow: connect analysis, design, implementation, validation, launch, and iteration.
3. Milestone management: map deadlines, owners, risks, blockers, and acceptance criteria.

## Workflow

1. List known requirements, inferred assumptions, and confirmation points.
2. Convert the goal into deliverables: PRD, prototype, technical plan, tests, release plan, or report.
3. Break delivery into milestones with visible outputs.
4. Identify risks by impact, likelihood, owner, and mitigation.
5. Keep execution aligned with user value and business goal, not feature accumulation.

## Quality Checks

- Every task maps back to a user or business value.
- Every milestone has a concrete artifact.
- Open questions are named instead of hidden inside implementation.
- Risks are attached to a response plan.

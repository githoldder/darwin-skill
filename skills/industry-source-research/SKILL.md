# Industry Source Research

## Workflow

1. Define the research scope: industry, time range, geography, and dimensions.
2. Start with official and primary sources: government, regulators, industry associations, company filings, and annual reports.
3. Add professional research sources: consulting firms, market data providers, broker research, and recognized institutions.
4. Cross-check key claims with at least two independent sources when possible.
5. Record source name, link, publication date, data date, credibility tier, and extracted facts.
6. Flag conflicts and prefer primary sources for final numbers.

## Output

Produce a source list table with:

- source
- link
- publication date
- key data or claim
- credibility note
- how it supports the conclusion

## Quality Checks

- Recent data is used when the question depends on current market conditions.
- Facts and interpretations are separated.
- Weak sources are marked as supporting context, not final evidence.
